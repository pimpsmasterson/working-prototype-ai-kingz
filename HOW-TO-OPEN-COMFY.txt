============================================
  AI KINGS - HOW TO OPEN COMFYUI
============================================

EASIEST WAY:

  1. Double-click:  OPEN-COMFY.bat

  2. Wait 5 seconds. Your browser will open to ComfyUI.

  3. KEEP the black window OPEN while using ComfyUI.
     (Closing it will disconnect you.)

  4. When done, close the black window.


IF YOUR INSTANCE CHANGES (new rent):

  - Open OPEN-COMFY-PORT.txt in Notepad
  - Replace the number with your new SSH port from Vast.ai
  - Save the file
  - Double-click OPEN-COMFY.bat again


WHERE TO FIND YOUR SSH PORT:

  - Vast.ai website -> Your instance -> look for "SSH" or "SSH Port"
  - Example: "ssh -p 32678 root@ssh2.vast.ai"  -> port is 32678
